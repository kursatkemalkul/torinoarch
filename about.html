01 — Design

Design Studio

Six disciplines, a single aesthetic strategy — from mass and plan layouts to forward-looking product design.

Architecture Division

Forms the architectural backbone of the structure. Starting from mass and plan layouts, it unites all technical disciplines under a single aesthetic strategy.

Interior Architecture

Designs the inner world of the space, its function-aesthetic balance, and user experience through a refined composition of materials and textures.

Exterior / Facade

Determines the identity the building projects to the outside. It leaves your architectural signature through material composition and environmental performance.

Spatial (Stand & Pop-Up)

Creates experience-driven, temporary, and memorable spatial installations (pop-up stores, display windows) for brands.

Visualization

Presents the architectural vision in its most realistic form; it brings the project to life before it is even built through high-end 3D modeling and animations.

Future Lab (Product / Drone)

Pushes the boundaries of industrial innovation with forward-looking product forms, mobility design, and advanced concept research.

02 — Build

Build Studio

Design and execution under a single roof — from turnkey delivery to public tenders.

Turnkey Design-Build

Unites design and execution under a single roof. With end-to-end management, it transforms the project into a flawless turnkey experience through a single point of contact.

Development Build (JV Projects)

Tender & Public Contracts

Executes all processes in private and public tenders, from technical/financial proposal preparation to contract management, with absolute transparency.

03 — Develop

Development Studio

From land potential to commercial success — analytical foundations for visionary investments.

Real Estate Development

Analyzes the true potential of lands. By determining the right product typology, it constructs the commercial backbone and concept of the project.

Feasibility & Financial Modeling

Clarifies the financial framework and feasibility of the investment through comprehensive risk analyses, cash flow modeling, and ROI/IRR calculations.

Investment Partnerships

Sales & Market Strategy

Reads market dynamics with sharp insight; it guarantees the commercial success of the project through accurate pricing, positioning, and launch plans.

Co-Founder · 01
Kürşad Kemal Kul
Principal Designer

Graduating with success from Mimar Sinan Fine Arts University, Kürşad is an award-winning designer who dismantles interdisciplinary boundaries. His experience with global automotive giants like FIAT and TOGG has equipped him with the flawless mastery of “micro-detail” required in industrial design. Today, he translates the refined aesthetic he cultivated throughout his career—spanning from China to Italy—into an architectural scale. Crowning his vision academically with a master’s degree in architecture, Kürşad masterfully navigates the fine line between form, function, and detail, orchestrating spaces with the precision of a machine.

She elevated her vision, shaped by the deep-rooted discipline of Robert College, to a global perspective through her master’s studies in London. Frequently Asked

About Torinoarch.

Who founded Torinoarch?

Torinoarch was co-founded by Kürşad Kemal Kul (also written Kemal Kul, Kürşat Kul, Kursat Kul, Kemal Kürşad Kul, Kürşat Kemal Kul) and Eslem Soylu Kul. Kürşad serves as Principal Designer with a background in industrial and automotive design (FIAT, TOGG) and a master’s in architecture from Mimar Sinan Fine Arts University. Eslem leads Creative Strategy with Robert College and London graduate-school credentials.

Where is Torinoarch based?

The studio is registered in Turin (Torino), Italy, at Viale Enrico Thovez 23, 10131. We operate across Italy and Turkey, with active projects in Istanbul, Ankara, the Dolomites, and the Italian Riviera.

What services does Kürşad Kemal Kul’s studio offer?

Torinoarch operates under three integrated practices: Design (architecture, interiors, facade, product, visualisation), Build (turnkey design-build, general contracting, public tenders, JV partnerships), and Develop (real estate development, feasibility, investment partnerships, sales strategy).

Is Torinoarch the same as Turin Studio or Torino Studio?

Yes. “Torinoarch” is our registered brand, derived from Torino (Italian for Turin) + arch(itecture). You may also encounter us as Turin Studio, Torino Architects, or Torinoarch Mimarlık — all refer to the same studio led by Kürşad Kemal Kul and Eslem Soylu Kul.

Where can I see Kürşad Kemal Kul’s portfolio?

Architectural and studio work is presented under Projects. Selected industrial and product-design work is shared privately on request.
