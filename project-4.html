55,000 sqm Pushing the Boundaries of Innovation in the Heart of the Metropolis

Lumina Tower is a monumental concept project redefining corporate life within Istanbul’s dynamic fabric. Spanning a total of 55,000 sqm and designed in 2022, this structure stands as a modern manifesto that unites light, art, and spaciousness in a vertical architecture.

The transparent facade, elegantly integrating into the Istanbul skyline, envelops a multi-story organization ranging from the grand entrance lobby to vertical atrium offices and panoramic ‘sky lounge’ areas. The massive whale sculptures welcoming guests in the lobby instantly reflect the plaza’s artistic and innovative identity.

Expansive glass facades and an open-office layout bring daylight to every corner of the interior, providing employees with a rejuvenating, ‘fresh’ environment. With its high material quality and fluid spatial organization, Lumina Tower leaves an exclusive and inspiring signature on Istanbul’s corporate life.
